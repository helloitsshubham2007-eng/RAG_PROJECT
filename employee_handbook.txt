EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK — ACME LOGISTICS INC.
Section 1: Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off and are reviewed quarterly.
Employees working remotely must be reachable during core hours, 10am-4pm local time.

Section 2: Paid Time Off
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year during their first two years
of employment. After two years, accrual increases to 20 days per year. PTO does
not roll over beyond 5 days into the next calendar year. Unused PTO above the
rollover cap is forfeited unless local law requires payout.

Section 3: Expense Reimbursement
Employees must submit expense reports within 30 days of the expense date.
Reports submitted after 30 days require director approval. Meals during business
travel are reimbursed up to $75/day domestic and $100/day international.
Alcohol is not reimbursable except at approved client dinners, capped at $50/person.

Section 4: Parental Leave
Primary caregivers are eligible for 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary
caregivers are eligible for 6 weeks of paid leave. Leave must be taken within
12 months of the birth or adoption date and can be split into two blocks.

Section 5: Equipment Policy
New employees receive a laptop and a one-time $500 home office stipend within
their first 30 days. Equipment remains company property and must be returned
within 14 days of separation from the company.

Section 6: Performance Reviews
Formal performance reviews occur twice per year, in June and December.
Managers must complete written reviews at least 5 business days before the
scheduled 1:1 discussion. Compensation changes tied to reviews take effect
the first pay cycle after the review period closes.
